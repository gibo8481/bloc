Nameplate

Download PDF Template

Time: 30 minutes

Level: Beginner

Supplies

  • Wood or acrylic sheet
  • Engraving tool
  • Paint or markers
  • Sandpaper
1

Draw Your Design

Sketch your design on paper or use design software to create a custom nameplate layout.

2

Secure Materials

Place your material securely on the engraving machine to ensure precise cuts and engravings.

3

Engrave and Cut

Use the engraving tool to carve out your design and cut the material to the desired shape.

4

Finish and Paint

Sand the edges for a smooth finish and paint or decorate your nameplate as desired.
