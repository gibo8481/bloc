Meet the Team

Bringing Ideas to Life

As makers and engineers, our team knows how exciting it is to make something from scratch. However, fabrication tools often require extensive experience or difficult-to-use software. We want to inspire the makers of tomorrow by eliminating complexity and dive straight into what truly matters—bringing ideas to life.

Commitment to Creating

We empower makers of all skill levels—from beginners to enthusiasts—to bring their ideas to life. Our gamified interface makes the creative process fun and intuitive, while built-in safety features ensure a risk-free experience and easy cleanup. Designed for versatility, our system works with a variety of materials, from everyday household items to wood and acrylic. Plus, our software grows with you, providing a pathway to learn CAD and integrate it seamlessly into your projects.
